    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to the most common questions about our lung cancer prediction system and how it can help you.

    • How does the lung cancer prediction system work?

      Our system uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze X-ray images for early signs of lung cancer. The AI model has been trained on a large dataset of medical images to detect abnormalities with high accuracy.

    • Is the analysis by your system reliable?

      Yes, our system has been rigorously tested and validated against medical standards. It provides highly reliable results, but we always recommend following up with a healthcare professional for a comprehensive diagnosis.

    • How do I upload my X-ray images?

      You can upload your X-ray images through our secure online portal. Simply click on the "Upload X-Ray Images" button, and follow the instructions to select and upload your files.

    • Is my data safe with your system?

      Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and use advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your data. Your medical images and personal information are kept confidential.

    • How quickly will I receive my results?

      Our system typically processes and analyzes images within a few minutes. You will receive your results shortly after uploading your images, along with a detailed report.

    • Can I consult with a doctor after receiving my results?

      Yes, we offer options for remote consultations with medical professionals. After receiving your results, you can schedule an online appointment to discuss your report and next steps.
